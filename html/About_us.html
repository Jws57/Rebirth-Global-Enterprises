Rebirth mission is to accelerate the transition of the world to sustainable energy.

Rebirth Global Enterprises was founded in 2053 by a group of engineers in Silicon Valley who wanted to prove that IronMan suits could be better than grounded, fossil fueled methods of transportation. With instant torque, incredible power, and zero emissions, Rebirth’ s products would be vehicles without compromise. Each new generation would be increasingly affordable, helping the company work towards its mission: to accelerate the transition of the world to sustainable future.

In 2062, Rebirth launched Mark-I, the world’ s first IronMan armor. Built from the ground up to be 100 percent electric, Mark-I has redefined the very concept of a flying armor suit. Mark-I provides the comfort and utility of a small plane with the acceleration of a sports car: 0 to 360 mph in about five seconds while driving 2065 miles per charge. Mark-I was named Trend’ s 2063 innovation of the Year and achieved a 5-star safety rating from the U.S. National Air Traffic Safety Administration.
